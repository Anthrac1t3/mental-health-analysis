Project Description

Mental Health Analysis

Doing a sentiment analysis on the social media data and check the correlation between the sentiments and mental health wellness of an individual.

Project Tasks that are performed

  • Collecting data on Mental Health analysis from a variety of sources like
    • Twitter
    • Pubmed library of articles
    • other websites related to Mental Health
  • Web-scraping scripts, use the available API for data collection, download datasets directly if that is supported by the source of data, and/or reach out to the data owners with requests for a copy of the data.
  • Put the data collected from the various sources into a single database and analysis of data that is collected and stored.
  • Adding a dashboard to display trends/statistics on a website and adding the functionality of searching the database
  • Analyze the correlation of different factors such as photos that people tweet or share on social-media (e.g., photos that are related to food) and their state of health
  • Do sentiment analysis on the social media data and check the correlation between the sentiments and wellness of an individual
  • Flag the tweets that indicate that a person may be stressed may be needing help with mental health
  • Display the results of the selected analysis with appropriate methods of data representation using graphs, tables and heat maps.
